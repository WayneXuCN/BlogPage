AstroPaper is a minimal, accessible and SEO-friendly blog theme built with **Astro** and **Tailwind CSS**.

AstroPaper provides a solid foundation for blogs, or even portfolios, with:

- Full Markdown support
- Built-in light & dark mode
- Clean layout that works out of the box
- Static search with PageFind
- Automatic social image generation
- Highly customizable design
